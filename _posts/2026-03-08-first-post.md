title: What Responsible AI Deployment in Government Requires
---

Responsible deployment of artificial intelligence in government institutions requires more than technical capability.

It requires institutional design that supports accountability, transparency and public trust.

Over the past decade governments have begun experimenting with algorithmic decision systems across areas such as welfare administration, healthcare and border management.

These systems introduce new forms of prioritisation and triage that shape the choices available to citizens.

Understanding these systems as **governance infrastructures**, rather than simply technical tools, helps institutions design more accountable deployment models.
